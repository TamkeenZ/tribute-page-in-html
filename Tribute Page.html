Ayatolla Ali Hosseini Khamenei

Supreme Leader Of The Islamic Republic Of Iran

Unity is our greatest strength, and division is our biggest weakness.

Early Life and Education:

  • Ali Hosseini khamenei was born on 19 April 1939, in Mashhad, Iran

  • His father Javed Khamenei and mother khadijeh Mirdamadi

  • Studied in Mashhad and later in Qom under prominent scholars like Ayatollah Khomeini.

  • Mastered Islamic jurisprudence, philosophy, Arabic literature, and political thought.

Role in the Islamic Revolution

  • Active opposition to the Pahlavi regime.

  • Close companion of Ayatollah Khomeini during the 1979 Islamic Revolution.

  • Played a significant role in shaping the Islamic Republic's ideology.

Political Career

  • Served as President of Iran (1981–1989) – the only cleric to do so.

  • Appointed Supreme Leader in 1989 after the passing of Imam Khomeini.

  • Has maintained a central role in Iran’s domestic and foreign policy.
